Our Story

Niyantri Cafe began as a small neighborhood coffee corner with a big belief: great coffee connects people. We source ethically, roast thoughtfully, and serve with heart.

Our Values

We prioritize ethical sourcing, sustainability, and community. Every cup tells a story of farmers & friends who care about quality and people.

  • Fair wages for farmers
  • Seasonal, locally sourced pastries
  • Quiet, welcoming spaces for work and connection
